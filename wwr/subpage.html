It Is A Thrill Of A Lifetime

If you think about it, every part of whitewater river rafting is an adventure. But, here’s the thing that most people don’t realize. It’s not only about what you see, but what you feel that also makes the adventure thrilling, well, an “adventure.” Adrenaline pumping through your veins as you crash through the rapids. The spray of the river getting in your face. The sudden feeling you get when your stomach drops as you go over a waterfall(even a small one.) No matter what part of the river you are on, who you’re with or what you see, every part is an adventure.
